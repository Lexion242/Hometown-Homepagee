Visit Drammen

Enjoy culture, nature, and food in this vibrant city.

Top three museums and galleries to visit in Drammen

Drammen Museum

Must see: The artifacts and the old houses.

Gulskogen Gård

Must see: The peacocks and the hedgemaze.

Galleri Athene

Must see: All of the paintings and the art.

Top three green spaces to visit in Drammen

Drammen Park

Must see: The Playarea and the fountain.

Bachepark

Must see: The channel and the Gazibo

Bragernesåsen

Must see: The cannon and all of the hiking trails

Baker Hansen
